MMD Loader for Three.js

A browser-based viewer for MikuMikuDance (MMD) models and motions, built on three-mmd-loader.

All files stay in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

This site hosts no models or motions. You are responsible for following the usage terms and copyright of every file you load.
